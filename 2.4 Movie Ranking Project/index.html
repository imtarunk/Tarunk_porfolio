My top 3 Movies of all time.

Aashiq 2

"Aashiqui 2" is a Bollywood romantic drama film released in 2013. Directed by Mohit Suri and produced by Bhushan Kumar and Mukesh Bhatt, the movie became a massive hit for its soulful music and heartfelt performances. The film revolves around the passionate and turbulent love story of two aspiring musicians, Rahul Jaykar (played by Aditya Roy Kapur) and Arohi Shirke (played by Shraddha Kapoor). As they face personal and professional challenges, their emotional journey is beautifully portrayed through mesmerizing songs and intense acting, making "Aashiqui 2" a memorable and unforgettable cinematic experience for audiences worldwide.

Fast and furious full series

"Fast & Furious" is an action-packed and high-octane film franchise that first hit the screens in 2001. Created by Gary Scott Thompson and produced by Neal H. Moritz, the series has gained immense popularity for its adrenaline-pumping car races, thrilling stunts, and an ensemble of charismatic characters. The franchise revolves around illegal street racing, heists, and espionage, featuring a group of skilled and daring drivers led by Dominic Toretto (played by Vin Diesel). The story later expands to include law enforcement agents, such as Brian O'Conner (played by Paul Walker), who becomes a key member of the team. As the series progresses, the movies focus on the importance of family, loyalty, and friendship, creating an emotional connection with the audience beyond the action sequences. Over the years, "Fast & Furious" has become one of the highest-grossing film franchises globally, with several sequels and spin-offs, each delivering more jaw-dropping stunts and intense action, making it a favorite among action movie enthusiasts.

Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani

"Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani" is a Bollywood romantic drama film that was released in 2013. Directed by Ayan Mukerji and produced by Karan Johar, the movie features an ensemble cast of talented actors, including Ranbir Kapoor, Deepika Padukone, Aditya Roy Kapur, and Kalki Koechlin. The story follows the journey of four friends - Bunny (Ranbir Kapoor), Naina (Deepika Padukone), Aditi (Kalki Koechlin), and Avi (Aditya Roy Kapur) - who reunite several years after their college trip. Bunny is an adventurous, free-spirited travel enthusiast, while Naina is an introverted, studious girl who has always had a crush on Bunny. As they travel together once again, old feelings resurface, and new bonds are formed, leading to a tale of love, friendship, and self-discovery. The film beautifully captures the essence of youth, friendship, and the dilemmas faced by young adults as they navigate their ambitions, relationships, and dreams. "Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani" received both critical acclaim and commercial success, with the lead pair's on-screen chemistry and the film's music becoming major highlights. It remains a popular favorite among audiences for its engaging storyline and memorable performances.
